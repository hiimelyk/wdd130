Content

Home page

Purpose: -I want to create an inviting page for people who click on my website. -On the home page I want people to see that I am 'human' and set a warm tone of who I am. -I also want them to feel connected and be interested in learning more about me. Content: -For my home page, I'd like for there to be a welcome message with my name and a short description of who I am. -I would want to write about my hobbies and interests outside of school and work. -I will also mention where I'm from and talk about my family background. -I also want to briefly mention my skills and some achievements. -The home page will also explain the purpose of the website and it will highlight more in depth of who I am. Visual: -There will be a picture of myself, my family, and maybe the school I am attending, and a couple photos of my hobbies and interests.

Images for the Home page

About Me

Purpose: -I want people to quickly understand who I am and my goals. -On the about me page, I want people to know that I have a sense of direction and that I am ambitious. Content: -In the about me section, I will have a short heading about who I am, and what my objectives and goals are career wise. -I would mention that this website serves as a portfolio and presents my professional journey and development so far. -I want there to be a button that leads to my audience to contact me somewhere near the top and at the bottom too. Visual: -I will post a couple of my current achievements. -I want this part to be easy on the eyes while still highlighting the important parts.

Images for About Me

Contact Info

Purpose: -Here this will be a section in my website that will have my contact information. -This is the sole purpose of my website, I want people who visit my website to reach out. Content: -It would include basic contact information like phone number, email, and maybe a link to a social media account. Visual: -I plan to have a small icon of a phone and an email. -Here I want to make sure that my information stands out and is easy to locate so that I can ensure that my website is effective.
